# Poetry Templates

## Haiku (5-7-5)
```
[5 syllables - image/scene]
[7 syllables - action/connection]
[5 syllables - twist/insight]
```
Tips: Nature imagery works well. End with surprise or deeper meaning.

## Limerick (AABBA)
```
There once was a [noun] from/named [place/name], (A - 8-9 syllables)
[Setup line rhyming with A], (A - 8-9 syllables)
[Short line], (B - 5-6 syllables)
[Short line rhyming with B], (B - 5-6 syllables)
[Punchline rhyming with A]. (A - 8-9 syllables)
```
Tips: Setup tension in lines 1-2, release with humor in line 5.

## Sonnet (ABAB CDCD EFEF GG)
14 lines of iambic pentameter. Volta (turn) at line 9 or 13.
